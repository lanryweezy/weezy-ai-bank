# Weezy AI: The "Impossible Now" Roadmap (100 Frontier AI Features)

### 1. Generative Ledger & Forensic Autonomy
1. **Self-Healing Double-Entry**: AI that detects a single-leg posting error and autonomously generates the offsetting entry before the trial balance is even run.
2. **Generative Forensic Deep-Scan**: A daily AI agent that "audits" 100% of transactions by re-simulating the business logic of every posting.
3. **Ghost-Leg Detection**: Identifying funds that moved without a corresponding business-event trigger.
4. **Autonomous Liquidity Rebalancing**: AI that moves funds between bank-owned settlement accounts to minimize inter-bank settlement fees.
5. **Real-Time Tax-Optimization Ledger**: Automatically moving portions of corporate revenue to "Tax-Deferred" virtual accounts based on current FIRS circulars.
6. **Synthetic Transaction Simulation**: Creating millions of fake, compliant transactions in a shadow-ledger to test the switch's limit before payday spikes.
7. **Neural Fraud Handshaking**: An AI that "negotiates" with the receiving bank's AI during a transfer to verify the funds' origin in <10ms.
8. **Automated Regulatory Change Injection**: AI that reads new CBN circulars (PDFs) and autonomously updates the `Fees & Charges` Python logic.
9. **Zero-Knowledge Proof Onboarding**: Verifying identity without ever storing the actual BVN or NIN on Weezy servers.
10. **Ledger-as-Code**: Generating the entire database schema dynamically based on the current financial year's regulatory requirements.
